Weddings & Events

Ballytrent House offers two distinct event options. If you are looking for that special place to hold a casual, chic wedding or party, we have a wonderful location overlooking the sea where we locate our marquee in our magnificent grounds.
Alternatively, we cater for smaller intimate weddings/events in our beautiful 18th century house (we can cater for up to 40 guests in our reception rooms). Small weddings can avail of our stunning private chapel (capacity 20 people).
Both events have the benefit and use of our award-winning magnificent gardens & ancient Rath.
We have a team of exceptional caterers who can deliver the menu of your dreams for your special occasion. Our guarantee is that you and your guests receive the warmest of welcomes, the best food, in the most elegant of surroundings in the unique setting that is Ballytrent House.
