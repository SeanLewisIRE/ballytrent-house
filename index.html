Ballytrent House

A warm welcome awaits you at tranquil and secluded Ballytrent House. Whether you are planning a grand wedding or a small exclusive celebration, we welcome you to visit the unique offering that is Ballytrent House.
With the ocean as your backdrop and set over three acres of beautiful gardens & lawns, Ballytrent is truly enchanting.
Located on the grounds is an ancient double-ringed Ráth, which is reputed to be the largest in Europe. The Ráth dates back to pre-Christian times and has a rich history of religious worship and ceremonies. Full of rare plants, mature trees and beautiful roses, the Ráth garden is a haven for songbirds and is a captivating location for a wedding ceremony.
